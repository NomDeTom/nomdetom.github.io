Control Pattern Glossary

Badge
Small label showing count, notification, or status (e.g., notification bubble on an icon). Read-only, non-interactive.
Button
Interactive element that triggers an action or navigation. Primary, secondary, or action variants.
Chip
Selection or filtering control. Can be single-select (exclusive choice) or multi-select. Solid or soft-fill styling.
Pill
Semantic status annotation inline with a value. Shows a property (OK, Warning, Invalid). Small, border-only style.
Tab
Navigation control that switches between different views or sections. Usually in a joined button bar.
Toggle
Binary on/off control. Includes checkboxes, pill switches, and self-labeling toggle buttons. Click to flip state.

Resistor Ladder Voltage Divider Checker

A standalone divider calculator for tap voltages across a configurable series resistor ladder

Formulas

total resistance = R1 + R2 + ... + Rn divider current = applied voltage ÷ total resistance tap voltage between Ri and Ri+1 = applied voltage × (sum of resistors below the tap ÷ total resistance)

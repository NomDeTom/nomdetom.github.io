Formulas

Symbol time: T_sym (ms) = 2^SF / BW_kHz Preamble duration: T_pre = (preamble_len + 4.25 sync word) × T_sym — the +4.25 is the LoRa sync word overhead (always present, independent of header mode) Payload bits: 8×PL − 4×SF + 8 + 16×CRC + 20×(explicit header) ≥ 0 — the LoRa header overhead (20 bits) is encoded in the first payload codewords, not the preamble Bits per symbol: SF − 2 (LDRO on) or SF (LDRO off) Payload symbols: ceil(payload_bits / 4 / bits_per_sym) × CR_denom + 8 Overhead symbols: 4.25 sync word — appended to the configured preamble length; appears in every packet, independent of payload size or header mode Time on Air: T_pre + payload_symbols × T_sym LDRO auto: enabled when T_sym > 16 ms (SX127x/SX126x requirement) Meshtastic limits (from airtime.h): hard limit 40% channel utilisation/hour; polite limit 25% Formula source: Semtech SX127x/SX126x datasheet; cross-referenced with ifTNT/lora-air-time
